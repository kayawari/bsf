Scanning Tips
  • Camera: Hold the book steady and ensure good lighting
  • Distance: Keep the barcode 6-12 inches from the camera
  • Angle: Hold the book flat and parallel to the camera
  • Focus: Wait for the camera to focus before scanning
  • File Upload: Use clear, high-resolution images
